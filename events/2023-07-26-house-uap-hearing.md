## Description

House Oversight and Accountability Subcommittee hearing on unidentified anomalous phenomena held July 26, 2023. Three witnesses sworn — retired U.S. Navy Commander David Fravor ([`/people/david-fravor`]), Ryan Graves ([`/people/ryan-graves`]) Executive Director of Americans for Safe Aerospace, and David Grusch ([`/people/david-grusch`]) former National Reconnaissance Office representative to the Unidentified Aerial Phenomena Task Force. Chairman Glenn Grothman of the Subcommittee on National Security, the Border, and Foreign Affairs presiding; Ranking Minority Member Robert Garcia. Documents entered into the record included a statement by George Knapp, a report by Jeremy Corbell, "The UAP Puzzle", and a Defense Intelligence Reference Document on Advanced Space Propulsion Based on Vacuum (Spacetime Metric) Engineering. The Subcommittee met at 10:06 a.m. in Room 2154, Rayburn House Office Building, and adjourned at 12:22 p.m.
