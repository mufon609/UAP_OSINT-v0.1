## Summary

News story with George ([`/people/george-knapp`]) and Dr. James Lacatski ([`/people/james-lacatski`]).

George: "A US intelligence official who designed and directed the largest UFO investigation in American history has confirmed at least one UFO type craft of unknown origin is being studied in absolute secrecy. And maybe at times we even go backwards."

George: "This is Dr. Lacatski's fourth and final book based on the AATIP files. I was co-author on two of those along with Dr. Colm Kelleher ([`/people/colm-kelleher`]).
