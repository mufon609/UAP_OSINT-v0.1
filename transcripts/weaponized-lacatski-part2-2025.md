## Summary

WEAPONIZED part two of two with Dr. James Latsky ([`/people/james-lacatski`]) with George ([`/people/george-knapp`]) and Jeremy ([`/people/jeremy-corbell`]). After ([`/transcripts/weaponized-lacatski-part1-2025`]).

Jeremy said "I am asking you about this craft. I am asking you, was it given to us? Was it gifted to us? Was it a crash..." Latsky said "you are going to get people mad at me for not answering when they know in advance and so do you that I can't answer." Jeremy said the craft makes the technology described in the book ([`/documents/inside-us-gov-covert-ufo-2023`]) look primitive. Latsky said it is "very advanced and with worldwide repercussions."

On disclosure: Latsky said the books "are my disclosure and they are the public's disclosure remember the public paid $22 million for the information." Latsky said 99% of the information from OAP ([`/organizations/aawsap`]) is "releasable" but the government owns the reports and they have to be cleansed.

Kona Blue ([`/organizations/kona-blue`]): "Kona Blue was a compartment reputed to be for recovered technology." Kona Blue the program was "in essence first described in skinwalkers at the Pentagon ([`/documents/skinwalkers-at-the-pentagon-2021`])" with column's ([`/people/colm-kelleher`]) private money suggestion. Latsky said OAP is not going to be repeated — "there is no way that is going to be repeated, that focus on the paranormal and UFOs."

On a legacy program before OAP: Jeremy said "you were read into and involved in a legacy UAP program before OAP. Do you deny that?" Latsky said "I can't speak to that. The only other clearance I had was a Q clearance at deal." Asked if he is denying it, Latsky said "I can't speak about that" — does not deny. Latsky said on the closed door briefings: "unless you have this compartment and are read into it and it is currently read in, I can't talk to you, Congress." Latsky said David Grush ([`/people/david-grusch`]) said that and Dylan Borland ([`/people/dylan-borland`]) said that and "everybody that has a knowledge in these special access programs."

On contact with non-human intelligence at a government level: Latsky said "I can't make a statement on that"; asked if he knows, "I can't make a statement on that either." On forged documents about OAP / Kona Blue, Latsky said "I have to say that I think it was deliberate false information put in there. Deliberate." Latsky said the OAP 10-month report was about 500page and they pulled them back from everyone they thought had a copy but did not number them and did not realize people were passing them around.

On the 2004 Tic Tac and Loheed Martin: George said it is the 21st anniversary of the Tic Tac and "if it is our technology, it is it is not our technology, is it?" Latsky said "No, it is not. I mean, I do not know that officially, but there are other things that were not discussed in the full descriptions that occurred that would have that implemented on aircraft. If we had that technology" — Jeremy said the Tic Tac is not Loheed ([`/organizations/lockheed-martin`]) Martin technology from 2004 and Latsky said "not that I am aware of."

On 3I/ATLAS: Jeremy said Latsky is a rocket scientist and propulsion expert and asked whether 3I/ATLAS is "a natural comet or possibly some mother ship coming to Earth." Latsky said "people are going to criticize me either way, but I think it is doubtful. It is almost like something we would do Star Treky like pre-star Trek pre-enterprise. It is just like really you are going to coast the universe and a big rock."

On personal experiences: Latsky said his Skinwalker ([`/locations/skinwalker-ranch`]) Ranch encounters were "mild to I would not say pleasant. It is just been mild. It is just a neutrality to it." Latsky said he had his own "man in black sighting" during Kona Blue, "not during Oset" — a woman on the express train from Union Station with a full-blown newspaper showing a "traditional typical always illustrated this way man in black suit, white shirt, black shoes and tie, shiny black shoes in the picture and a black hat" and two words below: "Join us."

On race for technology: George said Senator Reid ([`/people/harry-reid`]) "stated publicly several times that he believed that the Russians and the Chinese and maybe other nations adversary nations we are trying to do the same thing we are doing is recover this technology, figure it out, see if we could reverse engineer it and build our own." Latsky said "I agree with it is still on" and "as your friend Jay Stratton ([`/people/jay-stratton`]) has said you can't tell your friends without telling your enemies."

On recovered bodies and full capabilities: Latsky said "I know nothing about that right now" on recovered bodies. On the previous interview's statement that "if full human capabilities were known to us right now, the phenomenon is not something that we need to fear. our capabilities have never been fully revealed and we are still learning" — Latsky said "I stick with that statement." Latsky said it is "in Kona Blue aspects" — Kona Blue "is much more than going and getting what some have described as 60 year old parts. It is much more than that. And I can say I know what the exchange parameters were and I know what was promised on all sides."
