## Background

Alex Dietrich is a former F/A-18F pilot in the [`/organizations/us-navy`].
She was flying at [`/people/david-fravor`]'s wing during the November 14,
2004 [`/events/2004-nimitz-encounter`] while training with the
[`/organizations/uss-nimitz`] Carrier Strike Group.

---

## UAP Relevance

Dietrich is one of four naval aviators on the
[`/events/2004-nimitz-encounter`]. She had never spoken publicly about
the encounter until the CBS 60 Minutes broadcast on 2021-05-16 with
[`/people/bill-whitaker`]. In the [`/documents/written-testimony-fravor-2023`],
Fravor's Statement for the House Oversight Committee, Dietrich is "the
other pilot in my flight" who in 2016 contacted [`/people/david-fravor`]
and stated she had been "invited to the Pentagon multiple times to
discuss the event."
