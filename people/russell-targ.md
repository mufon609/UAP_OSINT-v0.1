## Background

Russell Targ is a physicist and author. Per Targ's ESP Research site, Targ was a pioneer in the development of the laser and cofounder of the Stanford Research Institute's investigation into psychic abilities in the 1970s and 1980s, and retired from Lockheed Martin as a senior staff scientist where he developed laser technology for peaceful applications. The 1974 Nature paper 'Information transmission under conditions of sensory shielding' (Targ and Puthoff) reports experiments at the Electronics and Bioengineering Laboratory, Stanford Research Institute, Menlo Park, California. Applied Optics author affiliations show Lockheed Missiles & Space Company (1991) and Advanced Technology Center, Lockheed Martin (1996), both at 3251 Hanover Street, Palo Alto, California.
