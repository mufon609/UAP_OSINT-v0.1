## Description

The All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office ([`/organizations/aaro`]) is a [`/organizations/dod`] office established on July 15, 2022 by Deputy Secretary of Defense Kathleen Hicks, following the enactment of Section 1683 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2022. Per the Hicks memorandum, AARO was established "to carry out the duties that were to be fulfi lled by the AOIMSG"; upon AARO's establishment, the Secretary of the [`/organizations/us-navy`] was directed to disestablish the Unidentified Aerial Phenomena Task Force ([`/organizations/uaptf`]).

The Hicks memorandum states AARO "will serve as the authoritative office of the Unidentified Aerial Phenomena (UAP) and UAP-related activities for the DoD" and "is the DoD focal point for all UAP and UAP-related activities". Its mission is to "synchronize efforts across the Department and with other Federal departments and agencies to detect, identify, and attribute objects of interest in, on, or near military installations, operating areas, training areas, special use airspace, and other areas of interest, and, as necessary, to mitigate any associated threats to safety of operations and national security." Scope includes "anomalous, unidentified space, airborne, submerged, and transmedium objects."

The Unidentified Aerial Phenomena Task Force was established on August 4, 2020 by Deputy Secretary of Defense David L. Norquist; the Airborne Object Identification and Management Synchronization Group ([`/organizations/aoimsg`]) was established on November 23, 2021 as the successor to the UAPTF and renamed to AARO in July 2022. AARO operates in coordination with the Office of the Director of National Intelligence ([`/organizations/odni`]).

Dr. Sean M. Kirkpatrick ([`/people/sean-kirkpatrick`]) was asked by USD(I&S) to stand up and lead AARO in early 2022; he led the office through December 2023. Timothy A. Phillips ([`/people/tim-phillips`]) assumed the duties of the Deputy Director in October 2023. Dr. Jon T. Kosloski ([`/people/jonathan-kosloski`]) arrived on detail from the National Security Agency ([`/organizations/nsa`]) to be appointed the director in 2024. AARO published its [`/documents/aaro-historical-record-report-vol-i`] in March 2024.
