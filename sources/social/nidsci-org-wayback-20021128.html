NIDS is Expanding Its Horizons

NIDS is evolving into an expanding organization as it increases its engagement with Aerospace related projects. In addition to focusing on high credibility aerial phenomena cases and on the animal mutilation phenomenon, NIDS staff and science board will now be expanding into a variety of projects and assignments that are directly related to Bigelow Aerospace. In taking this more proactive step, we are bringing the study of aerial phenomena to a tangible level. NIDS will continue its research into high quality aerial phenomena cases and into the animal mutilation phenomenon.

The National Institute for Discovery Science (NIDS) is a privately funded science institute engaged in research of anomalous aerial and related phenomena. We would like to receive all reports of significant close encounter sightings or unusual activities that are sent to you or your organization from witnesses within your area or local community. We use a combination of high quality ex-law enforcement investigative teams with contract research involving nationally accredited laboratories in the veterinary, biological, chemical, and materials sciences to research anomalous phenomena.
