USS Princeton (CG 59) Returns to Homeport
250813-N-WN039-1016 Gas Turbine Systems Technician 2nd Class Dinh Truong, assigned to the Ticonderoga-class guided-missile cruiser USS Princeton (CG 59) shares a first kiss with his spouse after the ship’s return to its homeport of San Diego following a nine-month deployment to the U.S. 5th and 7th Fleet areas of operations, Aug. 13, 2025. An integral part of the U.S. Pacific Fleet, U.S. 3rd Fleet leads naval forces in the Indo-Pacific and provides the realistic, relevant training necessary to execute the U.S. Navy's role across the full spectrum of military operations. U.S. 3rd Fleet works together with allies and partners to advance freedom of navigation and overflight, the rule of law and other principles that underpin security for the Indo-Pacific region. (U.S. Navy photo by Mass Communication Specialist 2nd Class Lordin Kelly)

USS Princeton returns to San Diego after 9-month deployment

SAN DIEGO – The Ticonderoga-class guided-missile cruiser USS Princeton (CG 59), assigned to Carrier Strike Group (CSG) 1, returned to its homeport of San Diego after a historic nine-month deployment to the U.S. 3rd, 5th and 7th Fleet areas of operations, Aug. 13.

USS Princeton (CG 59) Namesake Visit [Image 15 of 17]
230606-N-WF272-1268 PRINCETON, N.J. (June 6, 2023) The crew of Ticonderoga-class guided missile cruiser USS Princeton (CG 59), and Mark Freda, left, the mayor of Princeton, New Jersey, pose for a group photo in front of the Ionic Colonnade while touring the Princeton Battlefield State Park during a namesake visit June 6, 2023. The Colonnade is located on the Park’s western side and serves as an entrance for a memorial dedicated to 21 British and 15 American unknown soldiers killed in the Battle of Princeton. Twenty USS Princeton crewmembers visited the city to engage with Princeton-area residents, community leaders, local NROTC units and schools in order to further build relations between the ship and its namesake city. (U.S. Navy photo by Chief Mass Communication Specialist Diana Quinlan)

USS Princeton’s Sailors visit namesake city

PRINCETON, N.J. - Twenty Sailors, assigned to Ticonderoga-class guided missile cruiser USS Princeton (CG 59), visited their ship’s namesake city June 6-8, 2023, to engage with the community, share the Navy’s story, and learn more about the historical connection to their vessel’s namesake.
