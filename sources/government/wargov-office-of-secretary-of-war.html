Office of the Secretary of War

The Office of the Secretary of War (OSW) is responsible for policy development, planning, resource management and program evaluation. OSW includes the offices of top civilian war decision-makers with regard to personnel, weapons acquisition, research, intelligence and fiscal policy, as well as offices the Secretary establishes to assist in carrying out assigned responsibilities.
