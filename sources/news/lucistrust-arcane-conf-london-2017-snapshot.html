Arcane School Conference London 2017 - video archive

When

Saturday, June 10 and Sunday, June 11, 2017

1:15 - 5:30 p.m.

Where

The Ballroom,
The Amba Hotel (Charing Cross Hotel),
The Strand, London WC2N 5HX

Arcane School View program in PDF Video Archive

Keynote Let the group evoke the Shamballa Force and demonstrate the will-to-good in new and potent livingness.

Event Details

Video Archive

Saturday, 10 June

Afternoon Session – Everyone welcome

1.15pm

The Trumpeted Sound: A Formula for Transfiguration  Talk – Headquarters Group
 

“[The] new invocative work will be the keynote of the coming new world religion...  There will also be the skilled work of invocation by those who know the potency of formulas and mantrams. One of the formulas produces the transfiguration… At the very centre of the light in the head is an opening leading somewhere. It has been named the “trumpet through which the escaping A.U.M. can pass.”

2.00pm

The Way to Shamballa An Esoteric Catechism
 

Death the Great Adventure Irene Campagnolo Maschio
Irene Campagnolo Maschio is a Physiotherapist at Princess Alice Hospice, Esher.  Drawing on her understanding of the death process as regeneration and entrance into a new cycle of life, Irene will be discussing how she helps patients under palliative care accept decline while living as fully as possible until the transition comes. She works to bring awareness of hidden emotions and thoughts so that what needs to be considered can emerge and be shared.


Group discussion

3.20pm

Refreshment break

4.00pm

A Clarion Call: Music for Trumpet and Piano

4.20pm

The Rending of the Veils (Part 1):  Fairies, Devas and Unidentified Aerial Phenomena 
A discussion with Dr. James T. Ryder

Dr. Ryder is a retired Vice President of Lockheed Martin Space Systems Company and former head of the Advanced Technology Center. “The wide-spread use of electricity on the planet… will materially assist in the rending of the veil which separates the astral plane from the physical plane; the dividing etheric web will shortly be dissipated, and… the result upon humanity and upon the three other kingdoms in nature cannot be over emphasized.”

5.10pm

Group Meditation

5.30pm

Close

Sunday, 11 June

Afternoon Session – Everyone welcome

1.15pm

Headquarters Group
The “Light of Livingness”
“The creative ability of the future will emerge on a relatively large scale in the realm of creative living and not so much in the realm of creative art.”
 

Pluto, Vulcan and the Shamballa Force
Pluto and Vulcan are related to the Shamballa force and the first ray influence of the Will. Their influence is only felt in a positive manner upon the Path of Discipleship.


Meditation


Group discussion


Panel: The Spirit of Sacrifice – Making the Mundane Sacred
Two students discuss how they came to understand the true meaning of sacrifice.

2.45pm

Refreshment break

3.15pm

A Vision Comes Alive – The Science Behind Inspired Leadership Jim Grant
Jim Grant is an Executive Leadership Coach (PCC) living in Helsinki, Finland, and a Fellow of the Global Leadership Foundation with its Headquarters based in Melbourne, Australia. He will be sharing insights around how “Servant Leadership is emerging as Spiritual Leadership” and redefining success in business.
 

The Rending of the Veils (Part 2): The Violet Force and Health A discussion with Dr. Lee Blackburn

“The violet angels or devas are predicted to become especially active when the Christ Himself appears. Their activities will include: Instruction in the efficiency of violet light in lessening human ills. Concentrating man’s attention upon the etheric body so that the work and health of the physical body will become increasingly automatic.  Teaching humanity to expand consciousness to include the super-physical so that the separating web (the veil of the temple) which divides the physical plane from the unseen world will eventually be destroyed. The date is said to be imminent.”
