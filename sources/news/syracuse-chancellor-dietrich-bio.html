Alex Dietrich

Interim Chief Human Resources Officer

Alex Dietrich serves as Interim Chief Human Resources Officer, leading a dedicated team of HR professionals to provide strategic talent management and take care of people. 

She brings dynamic experience from a 20-year career as a Naval officer and strike fighter pilot, including multiple combat deployments for which she was awarded Bronze Star and Air Combat Medals.

With a pivot to academia, Dietrich served as faculty at the consortium of schools in Washington D.C. (Georgetown, George Washington, Howard, and Catholic Universities) and the U.S. Naval Academy’s department of Leadership, Ethics and  Law. Her last faculty appointment was as a Scholar in Residence at the University of Colorado Boulder.

A lifelong athlete and USA Track & Field Masters All-American, she stays active coaching, officiating and mentoring youth sports in the community.
