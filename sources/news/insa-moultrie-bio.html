The Hon. Ronald Moultrie

President/Owner, Ron Moultrie Consulting, LLC.

Former Undersecretary of Defense for Intelligence and Security

Ronald (Ron) Moultrie is the President/Owner of Ron Moultrie Consulting, LLC. He is an Appointed Board Member to both the President’s Intelligence Advisory Board (PIAB) and the President's Intelligence Oversight Board (IOB).

Ron brings unique leadership and advisory experience having served as the Under Secretary of Defense for Intelligence and Security. Presidentially appointed and Senate confirmed, he was the senior advisor to the Secretary of Defense on all intelligence and security matters and managed a portfolio of over $30 billion dollars and 225,000 global civilian and uniformed personnel.

In addition to maintaining total awareness of current and emerging global threats as risks, he was a member of the Department’s and interagency’s leadership teams that provided advice to the President of the United States and Secretary of Defense on issues of national security, Artificial Intelligence, Quantum computing, cybersecurity, and emergent technologies.

He was also the first Defense Under Secretary who was dually appointed to lead all security matters related to the Department’s 3.3 million personnel. This additional portfolio included ensuring the security of the Department’s physical, material, digital, and cyber holdings, leading the development of law enforcement policies, and overseeing investigative organizations such as the Naval Criminal Investigative Services (NCIS).

Leveraging his former top leadership roles at the National Security Agency (NSA), the Central Intelligence Agency (CIA), and the Department of the Navy, he also served as a senior consultant and Board member to companies and organizations.
