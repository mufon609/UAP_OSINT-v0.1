What is Remote Viewing?

Remote Viewing is a perceptual ability we all have that allows us to quiet our minds and see or experience activities in the distance or in the future. Fifty years of research and operational applications have shown that the accuracy and reliability of remote viewing are independent of both time and distance. It appears to be a kind of non-local entanglement for which three physicists won Nobel prizes in 2022. Physicist David Bohm, in 1993, called this “quantum- interconnectedness.” And in 1933 Einstein called it “spooky action at a distance. Find Out More

Russell Targ, A Pioneering Physicist and Author

Russell Targ is a physicist and author who was a pioneer in the development of the laser and cofounder of the Stanford Research Institute’s investigation into psychic abilities in the 1970s and 1980s.He most recently authored Third Eye Spies: A Guide to Remote Viewing and Transformation of Consciousness and Do You See What I See? Memoirs of a Blind Biker: Lasers and Love, ESP and the CIA, and the Meaning of Life. He is co-author of Mind Reach: Scientists Look at Psychic Abilities; The Mind Race: Understanding and Using Psychic Abilities; Miracles of Mind: Remote Viewing and Spiritual Healing; The Heart of the Mind: How to Experience God Without Belief; and The End of Suffering: Fearless Living in Troubled Times.He is also an editor, publisher, songwriter, producer, teacher, and more. Targ retired from Lockheed Martin as a senior staff scientist, where he developed laser technology for peaceful applications.
