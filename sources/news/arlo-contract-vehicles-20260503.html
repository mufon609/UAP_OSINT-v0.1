GSA OASIS+ 8(a), Small Business, WOSB, and SDVOSB

GSA OASIS+ is a governmentwide, multiple-award contract vehicle designed to provide federal agencies with flexible, best-in-class professional services across a variety of domains. It modernizes and expands the original OASIS program, streamlining acquisition with broader scope, standardized terms, and easier access for both agencies and contractors.

GSA 8(a) Stars III

8(a) STARS III, a small business set-aside GWAC, provides flexible access to customized IT solutions from a large, diverse pool of 8(a) industry partners. This next-generation GWAC builds upon the framework of 8(a) STARS II and expands capabilities for emerging technologies and outside the continental United States (OCONUS) requirements.
