Signature Series

Space Threats and Acquisition Forum

June 18, 2025

Lockheed Martin, Herndon, VA
TS/SI-TK//NOFORN

Registration is Closed 

Agenda

The Space Threats and Acquisition Forum (TS/SI-TK//NOFORN), part of the esteemed NSSA Signature Series, is the premier classified event dedicated to strengthening collaboration between the U.S. Government and the private sector. This exclusive forum provides a vital platform for in-depth discussions on evolving foreign space and counterspace threats, as well as critical space system acquisitions.

Featuring an unparalleled lineup of intelligence officials, defense leaders, and acquisition experts, this event offers cutting-edge insights into the rapidly advancing threats targeting U.S. space assets. Attendees will gain a first-hand understanding of the U.S. Space Force’s strategic plans and emerging capabilities to protect national security in the space domain.

Don’t miss this opportunity to engage with key decision-makers and stay ahead of the challenges shaping the future of military space operations.

 

Address: Lockheed Martin Space Dulles Executive Plaza (DEP) II 13560 Dulles Technology Drive Herndon, VA 20171
