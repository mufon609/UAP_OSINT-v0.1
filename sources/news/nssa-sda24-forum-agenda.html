Signature Series

Space Domain Awareness Forum

November 7, 2024

MITRE, McLean, VA

With the proliferation of satellites in an increasingly congested and hostile domain, Space Domain Awareness (SDA) is a central pillar in the establishment of an effective resilient national security space enterprise.  It is also one of five core competencies of the Space Force.  This event will bring together key US Government and private sector stakeholders to explore the most pressing SDA challenges and to highlight groundbreaking concepts and capabilities to address this critical mission area.
