## Description

Congressman Steny H. Hoyer (MD-5) ([`/people/steny-hoyer`]) press release of July 28, 2010 announced Military Construction projects in the Fiscal Year 2011 Military Construction and Veterans Affairs Appropriations Act approved today by the House. Funding will go to the Naval Air Station, Patuxent River ([`/locations/patuxent-river-nas`]) and the Naval Surface Warfare Center, Indian Head, Maryland. The Atlantic Test Range Addition at Naval Air Station, Patuxent River — $10,160,000 — was included in this bill at Hoyer's request. Funding will be used to construct an addition to the existing Atlantic Test Range, the only Naval Test and Evaluation Range on the East Coast, to house new control rooms for Unmanned Air Vehicles, intelligence data acquisition laboratory spaces, as well as Sensitive Compartmented Information Facility areas. The additional three projects were included in the President's Budget and strongly supported by Hoyer: Agile Chemical Facility at the Naval Surface Warfare Center, Indian Head ($34,238,000); BAMS Test and Evaluation Facility at Patuxent River ($42,211,000); and Tactical UAS Facility at Patuxent River ($5,500,000) — an Unmanned Aerial System Training Facility for the Army National Guard at Webster Field.
