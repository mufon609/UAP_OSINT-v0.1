## Description

David Fravor's prepared written statement submitted for the record at the July 26, 2023 House Oversight UAP hearing ([`/events/2023-07-26-house-uap-hearing`]). The statement opens with Fravor's identification as a retired Navy Commander and his November 2004 role as Commanding Officer of Strike Fighter Squadron Forty-One. The bulk of the document is a first-person narrative of the Tic Tac encounter: the 2 V 2 training context, USS Princeton's prior two weeks of Aegis tracking, the intercept itself at approximately 20,000 feet, the object's disappearance and reacquisition 60 miles east. The closing sections cover subsequent contacts from Jay Stratton (2009) and Lue Elizondo (2016), the formation of To The Stars Academy, the December 2017 NYT articles, and a call for Congressional oversight of any U.S. government programs involving craft "we believe are not from this world."

The document is a primary source for what Fravor stated in writing on July 25, 2023 for submission to the hearing. It is not independently verifying of the underlying encounter claims — those rest on Fravor's sworn account.
