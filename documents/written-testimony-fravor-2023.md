## Description

David Fravor's ([`/people/david-fravor`]) prepared written statement submitted for the record at the July 26, 2023 House Oversight UAP hearing ([`/events/2023-07-26-house-uap-hearing`]). The statement opens with Fravor's identification as a retired U.S. Navy ([`/organizations/us-navy`]) Commander and his November 2004 role as Commanding Officer of Strike Fighter Squadron Forty-One ([`/organizations/vfa-41`]), attached to Carrier Airwing Eleven ([`/organizations/carrier-airwing-eleven`]) aboard USS Nimitz ([`/organizations/uss-nimitz`]). The bulk of the document is a first-person narrative of the November 2004 USS Nimitz UAP encounter ([`/events/2004-nimitz-encounter`]): the 2 V 2 Air to Air training exercise controlled by USS Princeton ([`/organizations/uss-princeton`]) with Marine squadron VMFA-232 ([`/organizations/vmfa-232`]) flying Red Air; the training suspended upon launch for real-world tasking; Princeton's prior two weeks of Aegis tracking; the pilots' approach at approximately 20,000 ft; the object's disappearance at close range; and its reacquisition 60 miles east in less than a minute.

The closing sections cover subsequent contacts from Jay Stratton ([`/people/jay-stratton`]) in 2009 — identified as part of a program Fravor names "ATIP" led by Lue Elizondo ([`/people/luis-elizondo`]) out of the DOD ([`/organizations/dod`]) — Alex Dietrich's ([`/people/alex-dietrich`]) later Pentagon engagements as the other pilot in Fravor's flight, Elizondo's direct contact with Fravor in 2016, the formation of To The Stars Academy ([`/organizations/ttsa`]) with Tom DeLonge ([`/people/tom-delonge`]), Chris Mellon ([`/people/christopher-mellon`]), Steve Justice ([`/people/steve-justice`]) and others, TTSA's work with journalists Leslie Kean ([`/people/leslie-kean`]), Ralph Blumenthal ([`/people/ralph-blumenthal`]), and Helene Cooper ([`/people/helene-cooper`]) on the December 2017 NYT articles, and a call for oversight from elected officials of any U.S. government programs involving craft Fravor characterizes as "not from this world."

The document is a primary source for what Fravor stated in writing on July 25, 2023 for submission to the hearing. It is not independent verification of the underlying encounter claims — those rest on Fravor's sworn account.
